I am an undergraduate at Emory University pursuing a B.S. in Applied Mathematics (expected May 2026). My research lies at the intersection of **generative modeling**, **control theory**, **scientific machine learning**, and **inverse problems**.

I am currently working under [Prof. Lars Ruthotto](https://www.math.emory.edu/~lruthot/) and [Prof. Levon Nurbekyan](https://sites.google.com/view/lnurbek/home) at Emory, exploring connections between flow matching, score-based models, mean field games, and stochastic optimal control.

Outside of work, I enjoy working with sports analytics and running.
